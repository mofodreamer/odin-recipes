Dödölle

Description

Delicious and easy meal I used to make in the dormitry a lot.

Ingredients for 3 persons

  • 7 medium potatoes
  • 300 g flour
  • 1 large onion
  • oil
  • salt
  • sour cream

Steps

  1. Peal the tomatoes and boil them in as much water as it takes to cover them. Until then fry the onions on the oil.
  2. After the potatoes are boiled soft enough mix them with the flour while they are still hot. Make small egg-sized portions with a spoon and fry them brown in the oil left behind from the onions.
  3. Eat them with some sour cream.
